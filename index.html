About Me

I am a passionate Cybersecurity Professional with a strong focus on Information security operations, threat detection, and incident response. Over the years, I’ve developed a deep understanding of how to identify, analyze, and mitigate cyber threats in real-time, helping organizations stay secure in an increasingly complex digital environment.

My approach to cybersecurity is both proactive and strategic. I specialize in Web Application Firewall (WAF) management, API security, and cloud security, working to protect modern infrastructures against evolving threats. I believe effective security operations are not just about tools and alerts they’re about understanding attacker behavior, anticipating threats, and building resilient defense mechanisms. I place a strong emphasis on frameworks like MITRE ATT&CK, the Cyber Kill Chain, OWASP, and NIST to guide my analysis and response strategies.

Beyond technical execution, I enjoy contributing to team growth and operational maturity. I actively mentor junior analysts, develop SOPs, and create actionable security dashboards and reports that support informed decision-making. I also value the power of clear communication, whether it's in incident briefings, stakeholder updates, or team training sessions.

Cybersecurity is a constantly evolving field, and I’m committed to continuous learning. Whether it's staying ahead of emerging threats or adapting to new technologies, I’m driven by a genuine passion for securing systems and supporting business resilience through strong security practices.
